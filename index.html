Welcome!

Olivia Rando is a world champion martial artist, tricker, and member of Top Ten Team USA from New Hampshire. She's been training for nine years and travelling around the world to compete for four. Olivia is extremely passionate about continuing to learn about the art and teaching the next generation of martial artist. Follow her on her journey!
