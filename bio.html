About

Olivia Rando began her martial arts training when she was 5 year old after watching a local martial art's school performance. She began competiting year later when she was 9 years olf. Over the years of competition she has traveled all over the country and has gone as far as Dublin, Ireland for the WKC World Championships. Currently, she holds 10 NASKA world titles and 4 WKC world titles. In December 2016, she recieved her second degree black belt. At the end of the 2017 competition season, she was recruited for Top Ten Team USA.

In addition to her martial arts training, Olivia enjoys tricking, a mixture of martial arts, parkour, and gymnastics, plays the viola, and is learning how to code.

Olivia is a student at Stanford Online High School and a part of John Hopkins Center for Talented Youth (CTY). She joined CTY in the 3rd grade when she received high honors on the SCAT and later received high honors on the SAT, which she took in 7th grade.
